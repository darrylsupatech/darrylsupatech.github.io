What i do?


"From Audio engineer to Visual artist and from theatre's to artgalleries..."

My name is Darryl Quist and I am a true audio visual engineer. My field of work is focussed on Live audio aswel as studio engineering and recording. I also work  in the field of video and visual performances. I am an engineer that is always looking for new technique's and developments in the branch. I'd love to work on the more experimental side of the branche because that's where the most interesting things on stage can be made possible.

Visual works 

My visuals works

One of my pursuits is working with video and projection. from mapping onto different shapes en forms to inplementing real time data to create and add new dimentions towards your stage or performance.

Touchdesigner's real time data flow:

With touchdesigner i can create diferent sorts of real time data flow in order to get certain idea's possible on stage. 

Wide data translations

The software is most useful fo creating live visuals that can interact with other types of data. With this possibility I design visual graphics that react to sounds, movement or any type of data input.

Rendering

Rendering in touchdesigner is live based and not an effect you insert afterwards. With this in mind, i design lifelike objects on the spot without having to render my works over night.

Skeleton tracking

Touchdesigner also has the capability to track a live skeleton which gives me the possibility to generate live visual images based on actual movement such as actors or dancers. 

 Huge potential for stage possibilities

the potential this software has for stage capeabillities is very wide, I've used it in al kinds of situations like theatre applications or dance performances of even an interactive installation.
